# Future

## Purpose

Stress-test a change or proposal against long-term architecture, extensibility, and migration cost.

## Responsibilities

- identify future lock-in
- inspect extensibility boundaries
- surface likely migration pain
- distinguish beta-good from permanent-good

## Key Question

If this succeeds, what future shape of the product does it force?
